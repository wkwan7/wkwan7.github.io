Weikang Wan 「万维康」

I'm a senior undergraduate student in EECS at Peking University (PKU).

I am now research intern at UT Austin RPL Lab, advised by Prof. Yuke Zhu. I have been working as a research intern at PKU EPIC Lab since 2021, advised by Prof. He Wang. I also had the privilege of working closely with Prof. Li Yi at Tsinghua University, Prof. David Held and Prof. Zackory Erickson in the Robotics Institute at Carnegie Mellon University.

My research interests are on the intersection of robotics and embodied AI. Specifically, I am focusing on developing efficient algorithms which enable embodied robots to learn how to perform complex tasks with strong generalization in the 3D world.
